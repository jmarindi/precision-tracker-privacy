Privacy Policy for PrecisionTracker

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

PrecisionTracker ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the PrecisionTracker mobile application ("the App").

The App is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All fitness and location data generated by your use of the App resides locally on your device. We do not maintain external servers to store your personal fitness sessions.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide fitness tracking and mapping features, the App requires access to specific data points.

  • Location Data: We use GPS to track your route, speed, and altitude. This data is processed locally to trace your path on maps provided by OpenStreetMap and CartoDB.
  • Fitness Data: The App accesses your device’s sensors to count steps and calculate session statistics.
  • Media / Camera: If you choose to take pictures of your route, the App requires access to your camera and gallery. These images are stored locally on your device.
  • Email Address: The App does not collect your email address automatically. Your email is only used when you explicitly trigger the "Send Stats" function.

2. Data Storage and Transmission

  • Local Storage: All session stats, map traces, and photos are stored strictly on your mobile device. There is no background syncing to an external PrecisionTracker server.
  • User-Initiated Export: You can download your stats and send them to your email. No data is sent unless you manually click the "Send Stats" button.
  • Third-Party Map Providers: The App uses OpenStreetMap and CartoDB to render maps. These providers may receive technical data (such as IP addresses or coordinates) necessary to serve map tiles to your device. Please review their privacy policies for details.

3. Regulatory Compliance

  • Data Sovereignty: Since your data remains on your device, you maintain full control over your personal information.
  • Data Minimization: We only access the permissions necessary for the App’s core functionality.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access and Portability: View your stats within the App or export them via the email function.
  • Erasure: Delete your data by clearing the App's cache/data in Android settings or uninstalling the App.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions (Location, Camera, Sensors) at any time through your device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

PrecisionTracker does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). Since all data is stored locally, we do not have a means of identifying the age of users.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App. You are advised to review this page periodically.
